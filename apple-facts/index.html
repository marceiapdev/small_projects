Well known for their sour, tart taste. They are served as a popular treat after being dipped in smooth caramel. This apple was named after its founder, Maria Ann "Granny" Smith, when they were discoved in Australia in 1868.

Red Delicious Apple

Their skin contains more antioxidants than any other variety of apple. It's better to prepare them fresh as cooking them ruins the solidity of the flesh. However, they can be pureed and slow-cooked when making soups and sauces.
